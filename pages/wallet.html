Choose a Platform







Unzip it and move the folder wherever you want or drag the application MIMIwallet into /Applications or any other folder.

Launch the application. (If your mac complains that the app comes from an unindentified developer and does not want to open it, just right-click (or ctrl-click) on the app, and choose open > open)


The wallets you create or generate will be saved in your home folder. You can keep them there or move them wherever you want.

Make sure MIMIMoneyd and mimi-service are not running before you start MIMIwallet.

If you encounter crashes, open the activity monitor (in your app > utilities), and force quit MIMIMoneyd and mimi-service (if any is running) before opening MIMIwallet. (this bug is being worked on).

The log files will be saved in ~/Library/Application Support/MIMIwallet/.
